Free group sessions

We are now offering free youth-led group sessions to talk, listen and interact with your peers to see that you are not alone. These sessions are led by our young person volunteers who have had a battle with mental health and overcome. Here you can talk about any feelings you may have, ask any questions or simply listen. We also offer actvities during these sessions to help better understand your emotions.
