About Us

Who we are

Here at TheHappyPlace we are an organisation that works with children, young people and young adults who are struggling with their mental health. We provide you with the tools needed to look after you! We also aim to inform and empower parents/ guardians to be the best support system they can be for the young person in their lives.

How we help

It takes a lot of courage to ask for help, but when a young person does the support is just not there. There are thousands of young people today who suffer with mental health both reported and unreported and we are here to make that change! We offer a safe space where young people can find advice, tips, information and support to navigate through mental health challanges. We want to break the stigma around mental health in order to build a stronger generation that feels supported and understood.

We are here for you

Wherever you are,whenever you need, we are here to help.
